# Responsive Search Ad (RSA) Templates

Fill-in-the-blank headline and description formulas for nonprofit programs. Google assembles RSAs from your inputs — more variety = better optimization. Aim for 10–15 headlines and 3–4 descriptions per ad.

**Character limits:** Headlines = 30 chars max | Descriptions = 90 chars max

---

## Formula bank — Headlines

### Program name
- [Program Name] at [Org Acronym]
- [Program Name] — [City/Region]
- Join [Program Name] Today

### Who you serve
- For [Audience] Ages [X–Y]
- Built for [Audience]
- [Audience]-Friendly Program
- For Adults with [Condition/Descriptor]

### Location
- [City] [Program Type]
- South Bay [Program Type] (or your region)
- Serving [City], [City] & [City]
- Local [Program Type] — [County]

### Benefit / outcome
- Build Real Independence
- Real Skills, Real Community
- Grow, Connect & Belong
- Confidence Through [Activity]
- [Skill] Skills That Last

### Credibility
- 501(c)(3) Nonprofit
- Locally Based, Community Focused
- [X] Years Serving [Region]
- Trusted by [Region] Families

### CTA
- Learn More Today
- See Upcoming Sessions
- Join the Next Session
- Get Involved Today
- Contact Us Today
- Join the Waitlist

## Formula bank — Descriptions

### What + who + where
> [Program name] offers [what it does] for [audience] in [location]. [CTA].

Example:
> BCEF's Dinner Club teaches cooking, budgeting & life skills for neurodivergent young adults in the South Bay. See upcoming sessions.

### Outcome-led
> [Audience] deserves [outcome]. [Program name] provides [what] in a [setting descriptor] environment. [CTA].

Example:
> Neurodivergent young adults deserve real community. BCEF provides life skills & social connection in a supportive group setting. Learn more.

### Problem → solution
> When [audience] ages out of school services, [gap]. [Org] fills that gap with [program]. [CTA].

Example:
> When young adults age out of school-based services, there's a gap. BCEF fills it with life skills programs in the South Bay. Reach out today.

### IPP / funding angle (disability services)
> [Program] may be fundable through [Regional Center / IPP / Medi-Cal]. Contact us to learn how to use your [funding type] for [program]. [CTA].

### Waitlist / coming soon
> [Program] is launching soon for [audience] in [location]. Join the waitlist to be first to know. [CTA].

---

## Tips

- **Pin sparingly** — Google recommends letting it optimize. Only pin a headline to a position if there's a legal or compliance reason.
- **Don't repeat yourself** — if you have "South Bay" in one headline, don't use it in three more. Give Google variety.
- **Include at least one CTA headline** — "Learn More Today," "See Sessions," "Join the Waitlist."
- **Check ad strength** — aim for "Good" or "Excellent" in the RSA builder. Add more headline/description variety if it shows "Poor."
- **One RSA isn't enough** — run at least 2 RSAs per ad group so Google can test and the group stays compliant if one gets paused.
