# Activation Guide: Goodstack → Google for Nonprofits → Ad Grants

The full pipeline from nonprofit verification to live ads. Takes 3–4 hours of active work spread across a few days (there are two Google review waits baked in).

---

## Step 1 — Verify through Goodstack

Google uses **Goodstack** (formerly Percent) to verify nonprofit status. TechSoup no longer handles this.

1. Go to [goodstack.org](https://goodstack.org) and create an account
2. Submit your organization for verification
3. Required documents: 501(c)(3) determination letter + a recent bank statement or official document showing the org name
4. Verification takes 3–5 business days
5. Once verified, you'll receive a validation token

**Common issue:** If your org name on the TechSoup/Goodstack account doesn't exactly match the IRS determination letter, you'll get an "IRS Disqualified" flag. Email qualification@techsoup.org with the determination letter and explain the name discrepancy — they'll manually override it.

---

## Step 2 — Apply to Google for Nonprofits

1. Go to [google.com/nonprofits](https://google.com/nonprofits)
2. Sign in with your **org Google account** (not personal)
3. Complete the application using your EIN + Goodstack validation token
4. Submit and wait for approval — typically 1–3 business days

---

## Step 3 — Activate Google Ad Grants

Once approved, you'll see a Google for Nonprofits dashboard with available products.

1. Find **Google Ad Grants** and click to activate
2. Google will prompt you to create a Google Ads account
3. **Critical:** Choose **Classic / Expert mode** — NOT Smart Campaign mode. Smart mode locks you out of the controls the grant requires. Look for a small "Switch to Expert Mode" link if you only see the guided Smart flow.
4. Skip any billing prompts — there's no billing on an Ad Grant account
5. Note your **Google Ads Customer ID** (10-digit number, top-right of the Ads interface, format: 123-456-7890)

---

## Step 4 — Submit for final review

Back in the Google for Nonprofits portal:
1. Submit your Google Ads Customer ID to link the account to the grant
2. Google runs a final review (a few days)
3. When approved, your account will show the Ad Grants budget (~$329/day / $10k/month)

---

## Step 5 — Confirm the grant is applied

In Google Ads, confirm:
- Account shows Ad Grants budget
- Ads are eligible to serve
- No billing has been applied

If ads show "limited by budget" unexpectedly or are disapproved, check the compliance checklist.

---
