# Real-World Example: Beach Cities Education Foundation (BCEF)

BCEF is a South Bay LA nonprofit serving neurodivergent young adults ages 18–30 through life skills, arts, health & wellness, and career training programs. This is their actual Ad Grant campaign structure.

Website: beachcitiesef.org  
Service area: Manhattan Beach, Hermosa Beach, Redondo Beach, Palos Verdes, Torrance (South Bay LA)
