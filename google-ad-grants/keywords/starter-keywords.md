## Keyword best practices for Ad Grants

- **No single-word keywords** — "autism," "nonprofit," "arts" — all get rejected. Always at least two words.
- **Phrase and exact match only** — broad match wastes budget and tanks CTR.
- **Localize everything** — add your city, county, or region to keywords wherever it makes sense. Local intent = better CTR.
- **5–10 keywords per ad group** — more isn't better. Keep each ad group tightly themed.
- **Pause anything with QS below 3** — low Quality Score keywords hurt your whole account.
- **Review search terms weekly** — the actual queries triggering your ads are goldmines for new keywords and negatives.
