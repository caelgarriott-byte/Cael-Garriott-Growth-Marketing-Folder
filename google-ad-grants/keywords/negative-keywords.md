# Negative Keyword List — Apply at Account Level

Add these immediately. Negative keywords prevent your ads from showing on irrelevant searches, protect your CTR, and keep the grant compliant.

Apply at the **account level** so every campaign inherits them automatically.

---

## Universal negatives (every nonprofit)

### Jobs & employment (you're not hiring)
- jobs
- hiring
- salary
- job openings
- careers
- employment opportunities
- apply for job
- work for us
- job listing

### Free / cost-related (avoids people looking for free stuff)
- free
- cheap
- affordable (use with caution — keep if your programs are explicitly low-cost)
- discount
- coupon
- scholarship (unless you offer one)

### Wrong audience — children
- children
- kids
- toddler
- preschool
- elementary
- child
- pediatric
- baby

### Wrong audience — clinical/medical
- IEP lawyer
- attorney
- legal
- lawsuit
- diagnosis
- diagnostic test
- clinic
- hospital
- therapy insurance
- Medi-Cal billing (unless relevant)

### Informational (people researching, not looking for programs)
- definition
- meaning
- what is
- wikipedia
- research
- statistics
- history of

### Other nonprofits / competitors
Add competitor names here if you find them appearing in your search terms report.

## Program-specific negatives

### Life skills / Dinner Club campaigns
- recipe (people looking for recipes, not programs)
- restaurant (people looking to eat out, not enroll)
- cooking class (people looking for general cooking classes, not disability-specific programs — review if this drives good traffic first)
- cookbook

### Arts program campaigns
- art supplies
- buy art
- art for sale
- gallery
- museum
- art school degree
- BFA
- MFA

### Waitlist / coming soon campaigns
- enroll now (can't enroll yet)
- register today
- sign up now

---

## How to add in Google Ads

1. Google Ads → Tools → Shared Library → Negative keyword lists
2. Create a new list called "Master Negatives"
3. Paste all keywords from this file
4. Apply the list to all campaigns

Review the **Search Terms report** weekly (Campaigns → Insights & Reports → Search terms) and add anything irrelevant to this list.
