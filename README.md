# Marketing Playbooks

A collection of marketing systems, automation workflows, and campaign playbooks built for real clients and projects.

---

## What's in here

### [Google Ad Grants](./google-ad-grants/)
A plug-and-play starter kit for running Google Ad Grants for 501(c)(3) nonprofits. Google gives verified nonprofits $10,000/month in free Search ads. This repo documents the full setup, campaign structure, compliance rules, and optimization strategy.

Built while activating Ad Grants for [Beach Cities Education Foundation (BCEF)](https://beachcitiesef.org), a South Bay LA nonprofit serving neurodivergent young adults.

Includes: activation guide, conversion tracking setup, full campaign structure with real ad copy, negative keyword list, and monthly compliance checklist.

### [n8n Automations](./n8n-automations/)
A library of n8n workflow automations built for marketing and business operations. Includes ready-to-import JSON workflows for lead response, social media, UGC pipelines, review management, and more.
